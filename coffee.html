Good Coffee Places ☕

A curated list of coffee places I've discovered and enjoyed.

I like coffee. This page primarily has recommendations for small and independent coffeeshops; if instead you want inexpensive or mass-market coffee, it's not hard to find. For pedants, this page additionally contains recommendations for non-coffee items. There are many medium-to-large-ish chains, with varying levels of consistency and quality, for example La Colombe or Bluestone Lane. This line between "chain" and "small and independent but with multiple locations" is nebulous; I make no promises of semantic consistency.

Seattle ☕︎

Espresso Vivace has a founder who apparently invented latte art. This has been my first stop after multiple red eye flights.

Floret, in SeaTac Airport near the A gates, which has Stumptown beans and surprisingly good coffee for an airport. It's also a great option for vegetarian food at the airport.

In Bellevue, Third Culture Coffee is a good option, also with Indian-inspired options like filter coffee and chai.

In Renton, Boon Boona Coffee, focusing on Ethiopian and Rwandan coffee, is the best option. The cappuccino is not great; I recommend a cortado instead.

Five Stones in Redmond will make you a cappuccino freddo, which is not something you usually see.

Ada's Technical Books & Cafe (also in Capitol Hill) and Santo (a bit north of the U-District) are good. Victrola is also recommended.

India ☕︎

Note: Indians mostly drink filter coffee. You can get this basically anywhere, so this list focuses on fancier and more westernized (i.e., cappuccino) places.

Chennai

  • Backyard in Gandhi Nagar is a co-working space which includes unlimited filter coffee and chai.
  • Beachville Coffee in Alwarpet is a specialty coffee roastery and cafe.

Mumbai

  • KC Roasters in Bandra is the best cappuccino I have had in Mumbai.
  • Blue Tokai is a good default option, with cafes in a few cities around India. There are six Mumbai locations.
  • In Kala Ghoda, Kala Ghoda Cafe has good lunch options as well as coffee.
